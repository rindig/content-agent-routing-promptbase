## SCRIPT

**HOOK (0-5s):**
When Spotify recommends a song, it's not reading your mind. It's comparing your behavior to millions of other users in real-time.

**BUILD (5-30s):**
Here's what happens. Every time you play, skip, save, or replay a song, Spotify records it. Those actions become data points. It builds a profile: not just what you like, but when you like it, how long you listen, what you skip.

Then it finds users with similar profiles. Call them your "taste cluster." If people in your cluster are listening to a song you haven't heard yet, that song gets recommended to you.

It's collaborative filtering. No AI needed. Just math on massive datasets. Millions of users, billions of data points, calculated in milliseconds.

**LAND (30-35s):**
Spotify feels like it understands you. It's just very good at finding people who listen like you do. Pattern matching at scale is indistinguishable from intuition.
