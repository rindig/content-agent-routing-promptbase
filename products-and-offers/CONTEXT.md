# Products & Offers

## What This Workspace Is

The product and monetization hub. Content creates demand, products fulfill it. This workspace tracks what's being sold and how it connects to the content funnel.

---

## Folder Structure

```
products-and-offers/
├── CONTEXT.md (you are here)
├── audience-segments.md        ← Who the audience is, segmented by need
├── tier-1-digital/PRODUCTS.md  ← Low-cost digital products ($9-$97)
├── tier-2-courses/PRODUCTS.md  ← Mid-tier courses ($197-$997)
└── tier-3-consulting/PRODUCTS.md ← High-touch consulting ($2,500+)
```

---

## How to Use This Workspace

1. **Understand the audience** — start with `audience-segments.md`
2. **Check existing products** — read the PRODUCTS.md in each tier before proposing new ones
3. **Connect to content** — every product should map to at least one content pillar (see `../brand-vault/content-pillars.md`)
4. **Align with funnel** — see `../platform-playbook/funnel-architecture.md` for how content drives product awareness
