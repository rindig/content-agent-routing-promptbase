# Tier 2 Courses & Cohorts ($200-1,000)

**Audience:** The Builder — developers integrating AI into products, technical leads, smart business operators building AI-adjacent tools or workflows.

**Principle:** These products deliver the uncompressed version of what free content compresses. A viewer who has watched 20 videos realizes the free content is a condensed version of something much deeper. They want the full curriculum, the exercises, the feedback, the structure. The price point reflects the depth and the outcome — not just understanding, but capability.

---

## 1. "AI Fundamentals That Transfer" ($297-497)

Self-paced course teaching methods that work across all models and tools. The core promise: learn this once, apply it everywhere, even as the tools change every six months.

**What's included:**
- Prompt architecture from first principles
- System design for AI-integrated applications
- Evaluation methods (how to know if your AI implementation is actually working)
- The 60/30/10 framework in full depth with exercises
- Model-agnostic principles that survive tool churn

**Pricing:** $297-497

**Content Connection:** Pillar 2 (The Right Tool for the Job) and Pillar 3 (The Prompt Architect) videos demonstrate teaching quality and create demand. Viewers see compressed versions of lessons and want the full treatment with exercises and examples.

## 2. "The Builder's Architecture" ($497-997)

Deeper course on software design principles for the AI era. This goes beyond "how to use AI" into "how to architect systems that include AI as one component among many." For developers and technical leads who are past the basics and making real architectural decisions.

**What's included:**
- Software architecture principles adapted for AI integration
- When AI fits and when it doesn't (decision frameworks)
- Architecting around AI's weaknesses (hallucination, latency, cost)
- System design patterns for AI-adjacent applications
- Case studies from real implementations
- Advanced prompt architecture and chaining strategies

**Pricing:** $497-997

**Content Connection:** Pillar 4 (The Builder's Architecture) videos create demand from developers and technical leads. They see architectural thinking demonstrated in free content and want the complete framework with real implementation guidance.

## 3. Cohort-Based Workshop ($500-1,000 per person)

Live 4-week program where participants build a real AI-integrated project with guidance. Small group, high touch. This is the highest-engagement Tier 2 offering — participants get direct access, feedback on their work, and peer learning.

**What's included:**
- 4 weekly live sessions (workshop format, not lecture)
- A structured project participants build week by week
- Direct feedback on participant work
- Small group size (15-25 people) for meaningful interaction
- Peer learning and accountability
- Access to recordings and materials after the cohort ends

**Pricing:** $500-1,000 per person

**Content Connection:** All middle-of-funnel content feeds this. Viewers who have been watching for months and engaging with content are the natural cohort audience. They already trust the teaching and want the interactive version.
