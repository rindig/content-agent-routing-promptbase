# Brand Vault

## What This Folder Is

The foundational brand identity. Every other workspace references this. **READ-ONLY** — never edit these files per project. They're the DNA.

---

## Files

| File | What It Contains |
|------|-----------------|
| `who-jake-is.md` | Identity, credentials, earned authority stack, credential surfacing rule |

## What to Load by Task

**Don't read all 4 files for every task.** Load only what your task requires.

### Writing a script
| File | Sections to Read | Why |
|------|-----------------|-----|
| `voice-and-tone.md` | "Voice Rules for Content" (6 rules) + "What the Voice Is NOT" | Your writing guardrails |
| `who-jake-is.md` | "The One-Sentence Version" + "The Rule for Surfacing Authority" | Identity framing + when to use credentials |
| `content-pillars.md` | The specific pillar section matching your script's P-number | Pillar context and example topics |

### Choosing a hook type
| File | Sections to Read | Why |
|------|-----------------|-----|
| `voice-and-tone.md` | "Voice Rules for Content" Rule 1 (Calm Confidence) | The calm IS the hook |

Then go to `../script-lab/hook-system.md` for the 6 hook types.

### Brainstorming topics
| File | Sections to Read | Why |
|------|-----------------|-----|
| `content-pillars.md` | All 5 pillar sections (example topics lists) | Know what fits each pillar |
| `voice-and-tone.md` | "The Counter-Narrative" section | Know what Jake is NOT |

### Understanding full brand strategy (human review)
Read all 4 files in this order:
1. `who-jake-is.md` — who
2. `voice-and-tone.md` — how
3. `content-pillars.md` — what
4. `brand-story.md` — why (long game)
