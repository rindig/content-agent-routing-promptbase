# Content Funnel Map

Every piece of content maps to a funnel tier. Each tier has a volume ratio, pillar alignment, and offer connection strategy.

---

## The Three Tiers

### Top of Funnel — "I Never Knew That" (60-70% of content)

**Goal:** Maximize reach. Make strangers want more.

**Pillars:** P1 (Layers Beneath), P5 (Honest Take)

**Hook styles:** Quiet Reveals, Number Anchors, Counter-Positions

**Offer connection:** None. Zero CTA. Pure value. The absence of a sell IS the trust signal.

**Content types:** Animated explainer shorts (30-60s). Broad appeal, wide entry point.

### Middle of Funnel — "Here's How" (25-30% of content)

**Goal:** Convert casual viewers into engaged followers who see Jake as an authority.

**Pillars:** P3 (Methods Not Tools), P2 (Right Tool), P4 (Builder's Architecture)

**Hook styles:** Reframes, Question Spirals, Number Anchors

**Offer connection:** Implicit, ~1 in 3 videos. "I have a resource that goes deeper on this, link in bio." The video must be fully valuable without the CTA.

**Content types:** Animated explainers (60-90s), LinkedIn framework posts, longer Shorts.

### Bottom of Funnel — "Work With Me" (5-10% of content)

**Goal:** Convert engaged followers into customers. These people already trust Jake.

**Pillars:** Any, but framed toward demonstrating paid offering value.

**Hook styles:** Earned Authority Super Hooks, case study formats.

**Offer connection:** Direct but not pushy. "If this is the kind of problem your organization is facing, I help companies solve it."

**Content types:**
- Case study shorts (anonymized) — "A company came to me with X. Here's how we solved it."
- Behind-the-scenes of consulting work
- "What I'd tell a CTO who asked me..." format
- Testimonial-adjacent content framed as teaching

## Pillar Breadth Mapping

| Pillar | Starting Breadth | Entry Point Width |
|--------|-----------------|-------------------|
| P1 Layers Beneath | Broadest | Anyone curious about technology |
| P5 Honest Take | Very broad → narrow | Anyone skeptical of AI hype |
| P2 Right Tool | Medium | People making tool decisions |
| P3 Methods Not Tools | Medium | People past "which tool" to "how to think" |
| P4 Builder's Architecture | Narrowest | People actively building systems |

A week heavy on P1/P5 generates reach. A week heavy on P3/P4 generates depth. Mix both, weighted toward broader pillars.

---

## Offer Connection Patterns

| Pattern | Volume | CTA | Example |
|---------|--------|-----|---------|
| A: "Here's a problem, here's my approach" | 70% | None | Content IS the pitch |
| B: "Here's a method, it goes deeper" | 20% | Light | "Link in bio" after fully valuable video |
| C: "Here's what most get wrong" | 10% | None (implied) | Viewer builds mental model of Jake as solver |

---

## Funnel Health Check

- **TOF:** Are new people finding content? Is reach growing?
- **MOF:** Are casual viewers becoming repeat viewers? Are comments shifting from "cool" to "how do I apply this?"
- **BOF:** Are conversions coming from people who followed for weeks/months (not cold traffic)?
- **Ratio:** Is the mix close to 60-70 / 25-30 / 5-10?
